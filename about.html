Who We Are

Your trusted electronic component distributor

Est. 2011 in Hong Kong

Component Distribution Experts

CT Com Tech was founded in 2011 in Hong Kong as a specialized electronic component distributor. What began as a small operation has grown into a global supplier with extensive inventory and distribution capabilities across Asia, Europe, and North America.

We source components directly from manufacturers and authorized channels to ensure 100% authenticity and reliability. Our rigorous quality control processes and expansive inventory allow us to support the production needs of manufacturers across various industries.
